# Microsoft Purview for SMB partners

Purview helps partners tell a practical SMB data protection story: know where sensitive information lives, label and protect it, prevent oversharing, and support compliance outcomes.

## Training flow

1. Start with the Business Premium baseline: sensitivity labels, DLP, and message encryption.
2. Show the customer problem: sensitive customer, employee, financial, or regulated data is easy to overshare.
3. Demonstrate the MIP control pattern: classify, label, protect, monitor, and improve.
4. Attach Purview Suite for Business Premium when the customer needs advanced compliance and data security depth.
