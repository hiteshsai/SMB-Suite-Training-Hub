# MIP partner assets

Use this page to map partner assets into a partner journey for MIP.

## Recommended partner journey

| Stage | Partner asset need | Suggested output |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Learn | Explain why SMB customers need information protection | Short overview deck or landing page |
| Discover | Ask the right customer questions | Discovery guide and qualification checklist |
| Demonstrate | Show labels, DLP, and encryption in action | Demo script or workshop guide |
| Deploy | Move from interest to configuration | Deployment checklist and policy design worksheet |
| Expand | Attach Purview Suite for Business Premium | Add-on positioning guide and FAQ |

## Asset source

The partner asset catalog is tracked in [SMB partner assets](../../Assets/Partner-Assets/README.md).
