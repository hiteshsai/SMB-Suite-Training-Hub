# MIP POC-in-a-box plan

This is the starting outline for a partner-run MIP proof of concept or workshop.

## Objective

Help an SMB customer understand how Microsoft 365 Business Premium can protect sensitive email and files with sensitivity labels, DLP, and message encryption.

## Scope

| Workstream | Included |
| --- | --- |
| Discovery | Business data types, sharing patterns, compliance triggers, current pain points |
| Design | Label taxonomy, initial DLP policy candidates, email encryption scenarios |
| Demo | Apply label, protect a file, explain DLP policy behavior, show encrypted email |
| Readout | Recommended rollout plan, adoption steps, and add-on expansion path |

## Success criteria

- Customer can identify at least two sensitive data scenarios.
- Partner can explain which controls are included with Business Premium.
- Customer understands when Defender Suite or Purview Suite add-ons are relevant.
- Next-step deployment plan is documented.
