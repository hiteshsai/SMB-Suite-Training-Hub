# Microsoft 365 Business Premium

Microsoft 365 Business Premium is the base SMB suite motion for this hub. It gives partners a single starting point for productivity, identity, device management, endpoint security, email and file protection, and information protection.

## Partner positioning

- Lead with a single integrated SMB security and productivity foundation.
- Use Business Premium to anchor the conversation before attaching advanced Defender or Purview suite add-ons.
- Connect the value story to practical SMB outcomes: protect identities, secure devices, reduce phishing risk, protect sensitive files and email, and simplify compliance readiness.

## Included information protection capabilities

Microsoft documentation identifies information protection capabilities in Business Premium, including:

- Sensitivity labels for email, files, and Microsoft Teams meetings.
- Microsoft Purview Data Loss Prevention (DLP).
- Microsoft Purview Message Encryption.
