# Microsoft Purview Suite for Business Premium

Use this page for the Purview suite motion layered on top of Microsoft 365 Business Premium.

## Partner positioning

Purview is the "data security and compliance" expansion path for SMB customers. Start with the information protection capabilities included in Business Premium, then guide customers toward advanced Purview suite capabilities when they need broader compliance, data lifecycle, audit, eDiscovery, or risk management depth.

## Current hub priority

The initial hub content focuses on Microsoft Purview Information Protection (MIP):

- Sensitivity labels.
- Microsoft Purview DLP.
- Microsoft Purview Message Encryption.
- Partner-ready discovery, demo, and deployment guidance.
